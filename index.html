Ludvig af Klinteberg

Postdoctoral Researcher

Department of Mathematics, KTH Royal Institute of Technology

I have moved on from SFU, and am now a postdoctoral researcher at the KTH math department, as part of a fellowship from the Knut and Alice Wallenberg Foundation’s program for mathematics.

My background is in numerical analysis, with a mix of fluid mechanics, physics, and computer science. Most of my current work is focused on developing fast, high-order integral equation methods, for simulations of flows governed by the Stokes and Navier-Stokes equations.

As part of my research I have generated several bits and pieces of numerical code, which are available on my GitHub page. I am also trying to contribute to the ecosystem of numerical libraries for the Julia language, currently maintaining GSL.jl, FINUFFT.jl, and FMMLIB2D.jl.
